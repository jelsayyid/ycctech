Workshop

Claude Agentic Tools Workshop

A hands-on introduction to Claude Code and Cowork — Anthropic's agentic AI tools that are changing how people build, automate, and create. Designed specifically for non-technical students.

Presented by
YCC Tech Division Yale Claude Builder Club Yale CS Tsai City

What to expect

  • A walkthrough of Claude Code and Cowork and what they can do
  • Live demos from students who actively use these tools
  • Hands-on time to try things out yourself with guidance from experienced users
  • Food provided courtesy of the organizing partners

This isn't a top-down demo. Members of Claude Builder Club will co-present, walk you through things, and share how they actually use Claude day-to-day. Peer-to-peer learning: casual, interactive, and built around your questions.

This event is relevant far beyond the CS department. Whether you're in the humanities, social sciences, arts, or anywhere else — if you're curious about what AI can actually do for you right now, this is the session to come to.

The Yale Daily News will be covering this event. If this goes well, YCC has committed to a follow-up event later in April.
