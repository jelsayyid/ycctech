dreamboard

$40–$150 micro-grants for hardware components. 5-min application, 1-week turnaround.

process

  • apply. describe the project, list components with prices.
  • review. rolling. one-week turnaround.
  • parts. reimbursement after purchase, or procured through ycc.
  • ship. brief end-of-semester update.

covered

  • microcontrollers (arduino, esp32, teensy).
  • single-board computers (raspberry pi, jetson).
  • sensors, actuators, motor drivers.
  • prototyping supplies.

not covered: labor, software licenses, cloud hosting.
