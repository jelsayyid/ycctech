About the Initiative

Technology access is a student interest

The YCC Tech Partnership Initiative is the Yale College Council's direct effort to strengthen how students engage with technology as builders, researchers, and creators across every discipline.

We're not a CS club. We're the elected student government taking an active role in ensuring Yale students have access to the tools, platforms, and communities that matter for their academic and professional futures.

Through bounties, prizes, and speaker programming, we fund the infrastructure layer that makes student projects possible, and we make sure the people building it get recognized.
